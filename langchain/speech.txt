Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning are transforming the modern world at a rapid pace. 
From healthcare and finance to education and transportation, AI-powered systems are helping industries 
improve efficiency, accuracy, and decision-making.

Machine Learning is a subset of Artificial Intelligence that enables computers to learn patterns from data 
without being explicitly programmed. Popular machine learning techniques include supervised learning, 
unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning.

Natural Language Processing, also known as NLP, allows machines to understand and process human language. 
Applications of NLP include chatbots, sentiment analysis, machine translation, and speech recognition.

Deep Learning is an advanced branch of machine learning inspired by the structure of the human brain. 
It uses neural networks with multiple layers to perform complex tasks such as image classification, 
object detection, and text generation.

Large Language Models like GPT and BERT have revolutionized the field of Generative AI. These models are 
trained on massive datasets and can generate human-like text, answer questions, summarize documents, 
and even write code.

MLOps is an important practice that combines Machine Learning, DevOps, and Data Engineering to automate 
the deployment, monitoring, and maintenance of machine learning models in production environments.

As technology continues to evolve, AI engineers and data scientists will play a crucial role in building 
intelligent systems that can solve real-world problems efficiently and responsibly.
